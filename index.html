ABOUT THE TEXAS LASER INSTITUTE

QUALITY

of Service, Tuition & Support

The Texas Laser Institute is a result of a steadfast commitment
to providing excellence in training andcertification for aesthetic
professionals. With combined medical spa experience of
over three decades, our highly reputable instructors offer the
perfect balance of theoretical and practical knowledge in
providing comprehensive instruction and professional
development to its students.

We offer state recognized certification in Laser Hair Removal,
IPL Treatments, Tattoo Removal, Injectables, and Fractional
Laser Treatments.

The Texas Laser Institute is approved by the Texas Workforce
Commission as well as the Texas Department of State Health
Services to issue the prerequisite training and certification for
laser hair removal practitioners in the State of Texas.

TLI uses its access to advanced laser technology, its experienced
instructors, and its location near one of our nation's top medical
centers to attract outstanding faculty and students. The Texas
Laser Institute educates the new generation of laser
professionals for the 21st century.
